## I. Prefatory Distinction: The Drop Before the Drink

This record concerns Distillate.

It does not concern the Draught except where the latter is produced from the former.

This distinction is the first law of safe handling and the last law remembered by most thieves before their bones become steam.

The Draught is a stabilized longevity ration, medical sovereign fluid, noble restorative, battlefield resurrection adjunct, and legally measurable commodity of continuity. It can be catalogued by ampule, sealed by writ, tithed by house, diluted by apothecary, blessed by Cultus Vitae, stolen by desperate heirs, adulterated by criminal physicians, and misreported by noble accountants with the traditional courage of people who believe their grandchildren will inherit the evidence room.

Distillate is not so obedient.

Distillate is raw cineric reduction: the condensed, forcibly stabilized remainder of a Cineric entity, breach-borne organism, ash-phenomenon, high-order Bestiae mass, Conceptual vector, or other extradimensional continuance-bearing substrate subjected to necrological extraction. In common instruction it is said that Distillate is what remains when the impossible is made small enough to fit inside a vessel. This is a useful lie. Most vessels fail. Most rooms fail with them.

The distinction between Distillate and Draught is therefore doctrinal, legal, chemical, metaphysical, and political.

Distillate is the captured possibility of survival.

The Draught is the state-approved domestication of that possibility.

Distillate is the golden drop that fell upon Immog Aeter after the First Apparatus finished its work and became the wound around which the Concord grew. The Draught is the later compromise by which that singular violence was divided into sanctioned portions, diluted into administration, and made survivable enough that noble houses could pretend appetite was continuity.

Distillate is not medicine.

Distillate is not poison.

Distillate is not fuel.

Distillate is not sacrament.

Distillate is not weapon.

Distillate is the ancestor of all five, and therefore more dangerous than any single category written by a clerk who wishes to go home with his hands unchanged.

## II. Foundational Origin: The First Drop and the Crime of Singular Absorption

All authorized discussion of Distillate returns, eventually and unwillingly, to the First Distillation.

Accounts disagree in every detail that matters and agree in every detail that cannot be ignored.

A great Cineric entity was drawn into a trap.

The apparatus activated.

A force reached into the ash-periphery and seized the creature's inward law.

Witnesses described the entity dissolving as salt dissolves beneath rain, except the rain rose upward and the salt screamed.

Some accounts name the entity a High Lord of the Cinerum. Some call it a Cinelord. Some call it a beast too old for human names. Some call it a conceptual predator wearing the habit of flesh. Several later Mortis commentaries argue that all such terms are inadequate because the apparatus did not merely kill a beast but resolved an ash-law into a transferable remainder.

What remained was the First Drop.

The Muldunine Accord records it as golden.

The Orison Fragments record it as black at the edges.

The Sarantaran Counter-Register claims it was not a drop at all but a suspended knot of pressure, color, pain, hunger, and inverted gravity pretending to be fluid because mortal vision had no safer grammar.

All agree that it fell upon Immog Aeter.

All agree that it entered him.

All agree that it did not behave like fluid entering a wounded body. It is repeatedly described as a tide moving against injury, as blood flowing backward, as light seeking every opening, as a storm choosing a house, as a god being swallowed by a man and finding him insufficient but usable.

This event is not classified as ingestion.

It is not classified as baptism.

It is not classified as poisoning.

It is classified as Singular Absorption, Founder-Grade, Unrepeatable by law and inadvisable by reality.

The later distributed Draught protocol exists because Singular Absorption nearly ended the usefulness of the human subject even when the subject was Immog. It remade him, enlarged him, preserved him, and rendered death unstable around him. It also provided the governing classes with the most dangerous thought humanity has ever administered to itself:

If one drop could alter one man, then smaller portions might preserve many.

The Concord was not born when humanity won a war.

The Concord was born when it learned that victory could be bottled.

## III. Authorized Definition and Legal Categories

Under Mortis-Logistica Joint Handling Law, Distillate is defined as:

A condensed cineric continuance substrate produced by lawful or emergency extraction from ash-derived, breach-derived, or metaphysically discontinuous source matter, not yet rendered into medically admissible Draught, industrially licensed derivative, sacramental trace, or weapon-censured grade.

The legal categories presently recognized are as follows.

**Raw Distillate** is unstable primary extraction. It may be golden, amber, black-gold, white-gold, red-gold, translucent, mirror-like, or invisible except by the behavior of nearby instruments and dying animals. No color classification is sufficient for safety.

**Stabilized Distillate** has been acted upon by binding salts, marrow-lime, ashglass microflour, sanctified solvent, or other lawful alchemical constituent such that it remains within a vessel without active containment choir for no less than seventy-seven measured heartbeats. This is the minimum threshold for transport from extraction chamber to secondary preparation vault.

**Fractional Distillate** refers to measurable microscopic, powder-suspended, vapor-bound, wax-bound, oil-bound, or tincture-bound fractions prepared for medical amplification, noble minor restoratives, sacramental atmosphere, battlefield stimulant, or black-market dilution. Fractional Distillate is the most common form encountered by ordinary enforcement officers and the least respected by those officers until the first mouthful of illegal candle smoke makes an entire barracks hear their dead mothers calling from the cistern.

**Draught-Grade Distillate** has entered the Cultus Vitae and Collegia Mortis production chain for lawful longevity preparation. It is not yet the Draught until buffer, oath, seal, organismal compatibility measurement, house entitlement index, and Mortis sign-off are complete.

**Weapon-Grade Distillate** has been separated, concentrated, tuned, or adulterated for destructive application. Most such preparations are illegal even when produced by lawful organs. The exceptions are so heavily sealed that a quartermaster may have authority to fire the weapon without authority to read its ammunition label.

**Candle-Trace Distillate** describes quantities so small that Logistica formerly resisted recording them, resulting in several centuries of devotional fraud, noble panic-buying, and the celebrated Case of the Forty Thousand Consecrated Fraud-Wicks. Candle-trace grades remain legally real because belief in their possibility is economically measurable.

**Dead Distillate** refers to failed substrate that no longer produces useful restorative effect but remains metaphysically hazardous. The lower stack calls it grave syrup. Mortis handlers call that term punishable if spoken inside a vault.

**Hungry Distillate** is any batch that appears to seek biological contact, thermal stimulus, name-bearing organisms, memory-rich tissue, or proximity to ash-active persons. Hungry Distillate is not to be comforted, tempted, prayed over, warmed, tasted, threatened, insulted, or left in a room with an apprentice who has recently lost a parent.

## IV. Production Chain: From Cineric Source to Sanctioned Substance

The production of Distillate begins long before the distillery chamber.

It begins with authorization to harvest the impossible.

No legal Distillate exists without a source event. Such events include battlefield reduction of Bestiae Cineris, controlled termination of Cineric organisms within breach containment, shoreline harvest during Black Tide recession, recovered residue from sealed incursion sites, Conceptual vector collapse, and rare remains taken from the destruction of ash-cults whose bodies have become partially answerable to nonhuman law.

Each source category alters the resulting substrate.

Bestiae-derived Distillate tends toward mass, heat, hunger, and bodily restoration. It is crude, powerful, and more likely to produce muscular hypertrophy, fever renewal, uncontrollable appetite, and violent dream retention.

Conceptual-derived Distillate tends toward memory, compulsion, law, name alteration, identity bleed, and contagious interpretation. Its medical value is high and its administrative danger higher. A single improperly diluted Conceptual fraction once restored a judge's failing heart and caused every defendant in the chamber to confess to crimes committed by their grandparents.

Black Tide sediment Distillate tends toward multiplicity, corrosion, and unlicensed return. It is valuable in weapons and dangerous in medicine. Cultus Vitae bans it from ordinary longevity preparation. Noble houses repeatedly request exceptions. Mortis repeatedly records the requests so future prosecutions may be easier.

Cinelord-grade Distillate is theoretically possible, historically foundational, and practically forbidden outside apocalyptic writ. The First Distillation remains the only admitted Founder-Grade case. Anyone claiming possession of Cinelord-grade material without a Custodia seal is to be treated as liar, thief, cultist, noble idiot, or all four until the room explodes and improves the classification.

After source recovery, the chain proceeds through several organs:

The Eternus Legio secures the kill, perimeter, witness bodies, and surviving officers whose testimony has not yet begun to rewrite itself.

The Custodia Cineris determines whether the source may be harvested or must be burned beyond profit.

The Collegia Mortis performs necrological reduction, corpse-law separation, cineric activity measurement, vessel selection, and initial substrate coaxing.

The Liminal Office confirms whether the extraction has altered nearby shoreline behavior or attracted ash-periphery pressure.

The Concordia Logistica records declared mass, expected loss, transport seals, missing personnel, and the first of many lies required for the material to move safely through civilization.

The Cultus Vitae receives only what may become restorative.

The Ferrum Sanctum receives only what may become machine-stabilized component or forbidden weapon substrate.

The Null Court receives whatever incident produces enough death, ownership conflict, or name instability to require retroactive law.

This is the official chain.

The unofficial chain begins with the first person who sees the golden residue and remembers that every noble house, every dying industrial patron, every wounded legate, every addicted spire lord, every desperate clinic, every heretical apothecary, and every mother with a child wasting in a low-stack fever would pay more than a lifetime for a drop smaller than a tear.

## V. Alchemical Constituents and Stabilizing Materials

Distillate does not willingly remain itself within ordinary reality.

It must be persuaded, burdened, tricked, weighted, salted, cooled, sung, indexed, or morally insulted by the correct instruments until it agrees to occupy a lawful state.

The following constituents recur across sanctioned preparation and illicit derivation. Their exact ratios are sealed. Their existence is not, because too many dead smugglers have been found wearing their recipes tattooed under the tongue.

**Cineric Salts** are precipitated from ash-contact condensate and used to give raw Distillate a measurable edge against ordinary chemistry. Too little salt and the substrate evaporates into hallucination. Too much and the preparation becomes spiritually abrasive, producing burns on names, memories, and legal seals before it burns skin.

**Marrow-Lime** is rendered from specially certified corpse material, usually from bodies already entered into Mortis debt. It anchors restorative preparations toward biological reconstruction. The poor whisper that the noble houses drink the bones of the dead. This is inaccurate in detail and uncomfortable in principle.

**Ashglass Microflour** is ground from vitrified shoreline residue. It provides structure in suspension and allows Distillate to be carried in ampules, powders, waxes, and slow-release medical plasters. Ashglass contamination in lung tissue is a known hazard among candle-workers, relic-polishers, and illegal inhalant refiners.

**Black Tallow** is a binding fat used in sacramental candles, battlefield ointments, and certain low-grade pain-killing narcotics. Lawful tallow contains no more than trace fractional Distillate. Unlawful tallow may contain enough to make the flame lean toward the names of the dying.

**Saint Mercury** is not mercury and is not sainted. It is a reflective liquid carrier that allows fractional Distillate to be observed during reaction without direct visual exposure. The name persists because the first apothecary to rename it was poisoned by his own improved terminology.

**Aeteric Vinegar** is a harsh stabilizing acid used to prevent aggressive recombination in low-grade batches. It ruins flavor, reduces euphoria, improves survivability, and is therefore hated by illegal drug cooks.

**Choir-Soot** is collected from Liminal navigation chambers after high-exertion transit. It is used in rare tuning preparations where Distillate must answer to direction, motion, breach inclination, or shoreline pressure. Its use outside licensed facilities is a hanging offense in most districts and a private revenue stream in several noble ports.

**Obedience Wax** is used to seal ampules and ritual candles. Its recipe is contested between Cultus Vitae, Mortis distillers, and the Logistica seal banks. Most citizens know it only by smell: sweet, mineral, warm, and faintly like a chapel where someone has died politely.

**Red Iron Gall** is employed in weapon-grade scoring, shell inscriptions, and stability marks on distillate ammunition. It reacts poorly to lies. This is not metaphorical. Quartermasters are advised not to falsify ammunition logs while standing near open red-gall trays.

**Null Brine** is used to suppress interpretive drift in Conceptual-derived Distillate. It is expensive, unpleasant, corrosive to silver, and capable of ruining an entire noble evening by preventing guests from finding poetic meaning in their own suffering.

## VI. The Draught: Domesticated Distillate and the Politics of the Cup

The Draught is the most famous derivative of Distillate because the powerful drink it and the powerless are expected to die defending the supply chain.

This fame distorts understanding.

Distillate does not become Draught merely by dilution. Dilution produces poison, narcotic, false medicine, or expensive corpse syrup unless the substrate is properly reconciled to the intended body.

A lawful Draught course requires declared source, mortality index, house entitlement, compatibility assay, humoral breach-read, Mortis purity confirmation, Cultus Vitae application writ, Logistica ampule count, and succession conflict review. In practice, noble houses attempt to accelerate, bribe, blackmail, inherit, steal, counterfeit, or litigate every step.

The Draught exists because raw Distillate cannot be safely borne by most bodies. The First Drop chose, overwhelmed, or ruined Immog Aeter according to the theology preferred by the speaker. Later humanity did not have the luxury of repeating the miracle at full strength. It therefore learned the smaller sin.

Draught preparation breaks Distillate into rationed continuance.

It teaches stolen survival to enter the body slowly.

It binds impossible vitality to blood, bone, nerve, memory, appetite, legal identity, and the measurable greed of those who can afford recurring treatment.

A single lawful dose may restore organ function, repair catastrophic wounds, slow senescence, stabilize Reversion, strengthen immune response, delay ash-sickness, or prolong noble rule well past the point at which ordinary civic decency would have allowed a funeral.

Repeated courses produce dependence.

Dependence produces house policy.

House policy produces war.

The Concord's official position is that the Draught is a controlled medical and continuity substance necessary to preserve strategic leadership, rare expertise, and civilization's institutional memory.

The lower-stack position is that the rich found a way to drink the end of the world and invoice the poor for the cup.

Both statements are admissible in sealed court. Only one is safe to print.

## VIII. Narcotic Economies: Rich Man's Eternity and Poor Man's Warmth

Distillate is valuable because it alters the boundary between life and death.

That boundary is the oldest market.

The wealthy consume fractional derivatives to polish decline into elegance. They inhale candle-trace vapors during mourning dinners. They bathe in weak restorative salts. They commission scented oils containing legally immeasurable fragments of golden ash. They purchase night-drops that make old nerves feel young, memory cordials that allow them to relive victories more vividly than they lived them, and appetite suppressants that spare them the indignity of ordinary bodily need.

The poor consume derivatives to endure what the wealthy created.

A dockworker takes longwake to unload ration barges for thirty-six hours during famine rerouting. A heat-duct child inhales saint smoke to stop feeling the crack in his ribs. A penal laborer chews grave-sugar to keep carrying contaminated corpses after the gloves fail. A lower-stack mother buys one illegal warm mercy ampule and divides it between three children, knowing the dosage is too small to cure and large enough to dream.

This is why enforcement never eliminates the trade.

The trade does not survive because criminals are clever.

It survives because pain is punctual, labor is required, and lawful medicine arrives with paperwork longer than the fever.

Distillate-derived narcotics are classified by dominant effect.

**Brighteners** heighten perception, memory, reflex, and pattern recognition. They are favored by navigational assistants, illegal duelists, account-war clerks, assassination watchers, and students of noble academies who insist their success is natural.

**Warmers** create sensations of safety, heat, bodily presence, and temporary grief relief. They are common in cold districts, ash-shadow housing, Mortis labor barracks, and Liminal-adjacent ports. Some Warmers are made from candle-trace distillate. Others are made from desperation and lamp oil.

**Binders** reduce panic, suppress personality fragmentation, and help ash-exposed workers continue functioning after witnessing events that should have ended the self. Proper Binders are medicine. Illegal Binders create obedient workers who cannot remember why obedience hurts.

**Hunger-Drops** stimulate appetite, tissue repair, aggression, sexual function, and survival mania. They are prized by failing nobles and pit fighters. Excess use produces teeth dreams, meat fixation, and the conviction that nearby bodies contain borrowed years.

**Silencers** suppress pain. Legal Silencers allow surgery. Illegal Silencers allow employers to discover how long a body can keep working after it should have objected.

**Returners** produce euphoria associated with the feeling of having narrowly avoided death. They are highly addictive among soldiers, duelists, smugglers, and noble heirs who have mistaken survival for personality.

No narcotic category is harmless.

Every derivative teaches the user a small falsehood about mortality.

Some users recover.

Some seek stronger lessons.

Some begin saving candle stubs in locked boxes and whispering gratitude to whatever died inside the flame.

## IX. Sacramental Trace: Candles, Incense, Perfume, and the Economics of Possibility

The Cultus Vitae denies that ordinary devotional candles are meaningful Draught substitutes.

This is technically true.

The Cultus Vitae also permits certain high feast candles, reliquary perfumes, deathbed censers, saint oils, and consecrated votive tapers to contain trace fractional Distillate below restorative threshold.

This is also technically true.

Between these truths lies one of the largest devotional economies in the Concord.

A single particle of lawful Distillate within a thousand-weight batch of black tallow is enough to allow the seller to imply proximity to miracle. A dying grandmother cannot drink the Draught, but her family may burn a candle and believe the air remembers how bodies are restored. A wounded veteran may never receive noble medicine, but he may inhale chapel smoke and feel warmth behind the scar. A noble house may publicly condemn vulgar superstition while privately purchasing entire vaults of feast candles after a bad succession omen.

Most holy candles contain no useful quantity.

Some contain none at all.

A few contain enough to matter.

That uncertainty is the product.

The Church-aligned distributors speak of hope.

Logistica speaks of trace inventory.

Mortis speaks of substrate leakage.

Null Court speaks of fraud only when fraud becomes politically inconvenient.

In the lower stacks, people burn candles by the hundreds and thousands, not because they believe each flame is a cure, but because arithmetic becomes theology when the lawful world has denied all other medicine.

There are recorded cases of mass candle vigils producing measurable restorative anomalies. There are more recorded cases of suffocation, ashglass lung, tallow poisoning, candle-house fires, and entire tenement blocks rendered legally suspicious because someone bought consecrated wax from a man whose cousin once swept a Mortis floor.

The chance is enough.

The chance is always enough.

That is why the trade cannot be killed.

## X. Industrial Uses and Machine-Adjacent Applications

Distillate is not only consumed by bodies.

It is also consumed by systems that would prefer not to admit the resemblance.

The Ferrum Sanctum licenses a narrow range of machine-adjacent distillate applications under intense censure. These include self-repairing seal matrices, continuity-preserving archive locks, emergency vessel-start cartridges for ash-damaged engines, restorative bath solutions for ancient mechanical organs, high-memory substrate coatings, and certain forms of corpse-responsive diagnostic machine used by the Collegia Mortis.

The Undying Lords have attempted every conceivable unauthorized application.

They have mixed Distillate into nerve gel, cognition oil, joint suspension, sensory lace, memory vault coolant, electro-hollow capacitor baths, Shidnan mapping inks, wet-core preservation broths, and servo-muscular growth substrates. Some experiments succeeded. Some produced house patriarchs who could remember the names of screws but not their children. Some produced machines that screamed when disassembled. Ferrum doctrine condemns any process that encourages machinery to develop appetite, memory preference, self-preservation beyond instruction, name response, dream retention, or reproductive process.

The Undying Lords reply that survival without adaptation is merely slow disposal.

The Ferrum replies with warrants.

Distillate also appears in industrial emergency rites. A failing manufactory heart may be dosed with dead Distillate to buy six hours of output during siege. A water purifier may be sealed with ashglass and fractional substrate to resist Black Tide contamination. A transit lift serving an entire lower district may receive a forbidden warm-start ampule if the alternative is famine riot, though every official present will later claim the ampule was saline, lubricant, prayer-water, or someone else's handwriting.

Such measures are always exceptions.

The Concord is built from exceptions stacked until they resemble policy.

## XI. Weapon-Grade Distillate: Humanity's Most Advanced Cruelty

Weapon-grade Distillate exists because humanity discovered that a substance capable of persuading life to remain could also persuade other things to stop being coherent.

No ordinary weapon using Distillate is ordinary after the first firing.

The safest category, if safety can be insulted so broadly, consists of **Distillate-Tipped Munitions**. These are small arms, heavy rounds, penetrator darts, or execution slugs carrying microscopic substrate charges tuned to disrupt ash-derived regeneration, Bestiae tissue cohesion, Reversion stability, or Conceptual anchoring. Used properly, they prevent things from healing. Used improperly, they encourage the target to heal into something that regards bullets as reproductive advice.

**Cineric Lances** use stabilized Distillate in a focused energetic discharge that burns along the boundary between body and continuance. They are effective against high-grade ash entities, armored immortality infrastructure, and certain undead noble chassis. They are also effective against walls, witnesses, evidence, and the career of anyone who signs them out without sufficient seal chain.

**Golden Phage Charges** seed a target area with hostile restorative contradiction. Flesh attempts to rebuild in mutually exclusive patterns. Bestiae bodies forget which injuries are original. Conceptual growths lose interpretive stability. Human victims are not to be described in public reports.

**Ash-Cancellation Bombards** use Distillate bound with Null Brine and red iron gall to collapse small breach phenomena, interrupt Black Tide tendrils, and sterilize shoreline protrusions. Their use is strategic, rare, and followed by decades of litigation from anyone downwind who develops second memories.

**Reversion Hooks** are Cohors Noctis and Custodia Cineris devices designed to pull unstable immortals, traitorous continuants, or distillate-corrupted officers back into vulnerable embodiment long enough for execution. They are considered merciful because the alternatives are more expensive.

**Draught-Denial Blades** are ceremonial only in the way that an execution scaffold is architectural. Their edges are treated to interrupt noble restorative response and prevent immediate continuity intervention. Officially they are restricted to Null Court and Cohors Noctis use. Unofficially every old house fears them enough to buy counterfeits.

**Vitae Fire** is a forbidden incendiary made by combining dead Distillate, black tallow, ashglass, and volatile carrier. It clings to living tissue, machine housing, and certain legal documents with equal enthusiasm. It is banned in spire warfare after the Lyran Stair event, permitted in dead-world reclamation, and privately stockpiled by commanders who insist they would never use it unless history required them to be misunderstood.

**Continuance Mines** do not kill immediately. They mark survivors. The mark may later prevent healing, attract ash predators, trigger Reversion, compromise ident stability, or render a target visible to Liminal senses. They are hated by soldiers because a man may walk away believing himself lucky and discover months later that the mine was patient.

**Founder-Class Weapons** are rumored, denied, sealed, and budgeted under names that mean nothing. They are said to use Distillate not as payload but as interpretive command, forcing an enemy formation, beast, city, or shoreline to accept a revised definition of what it is. The Custodia Veritatis insists no such weapons are deployed. The Concordia Logistica maintains warehouses that officially contain agricultural replacement parts and require Aeteric Guard protection.

Weapon-grade Distillate represents humanity at its most brilliant and least defensible.

It is proof that the species can turn stolen immortality into ammunition before it can distribute clean water without a tribunal.

## XII. Custody, Theft, and the Black Market of the Immortal Drop

Every unit of lawful Distillate generates paperwork before it exists.

Harvest prediction, extraction authorization, battlefield claim, Mortis acceptance, Logistica movement, Cultus allocation, Ferrum audit, Null Court dispute reserve, noble entitlement, and waste disposal must all be recorded. The mass lost to process is expected. The mass lost to theft is expected. The mass lost to measurement disagreement is expected. The mass lost to an official's nephew is never expected until it happens every century.

The black market operates through leak, salvage, counterfeit, and inheritance.

Leak occurs when fractional quantities are siphoned from legal chains. A handler wipes an ampule. A candle-house receives richer tallow than declared. A battlefield medic keeps a broken seal. A noble apothecary misreports evaporation. A Mortis apprentice hides residue under a fingernail and loses the finger, then the hand, then the name.

Salvage occurs after battles, shoreline events, clinic fires, noble assassinations, failed distillery reactions, and transport disasters. Salvagers hunt for stained cloth, gold-crusted glass, treated bandages, used ampules, corpse wax, contaminated bullets, reliquary ash, and the teeth of people who drank illegal derivatives.

Counterfeit is the largest market by volume. Most counterfeit Distillate contains no Distillate. This is fortunate for public health and unfortunate for public faith. Counterfeiters use gilded oils, stimulant acids, ashglass dust, animal marrow, low-grade narcotics, phosphorescent fungus, and powdered relic fraud. The penalty for counterfeit continuity substrate is severe. The profit is worse.

Inheritance theft occurs within noble houses. A dying lord's dose is diluted. A younger heir receives a stronger course than declared. A spouse replaces compatible Draught with bright poison. A house physician keeps the difference. A still-living patriarch discovers his family has begun auctioning his future ampules by the decade.

Because Distillate alters life, every theft becomes more than property crime.

It becomes attempted murder, attempted resurrection, succession interference, treason against strategic expertise, unlawful mercy, unlawful hope, or unauthorized continuation.

The Null Court enjoys such distinctions.

The dead do not.

## XIII. Contamination and Exposure

Distillate exposure is not measured solely by dose.

It is measured by route, source, emotional state, proximity to ash, legal identity stability, heat, memory burden, and whether the exposed person has recently been named in a will.

Skin exposure may produce burns, healing, numbness, gold-veining, scar reversal, or the sudden reopening of old wounds that believe the body has become negotiable.

Inhalation may produce warmth, euphoria, grief events, ancestral hallucination, black tears, ash-cough, compulsive prayer, or temporary ability to hear machinery remembering its first activation.

Ingestion may produce restoration, organ failure, hunger, youth fever, memory duplication, name drift, violent empathy, death resistance, or a calm desire to return to the place where the source creature died.

Injection is almost always criminal outside sanctioned medical use. It is favored by desperate nobles, pit surgeons, battlefield cults, and idiots with access to silver needles.

Ocular exposure is treated as a Mortis emergency. The eye is a poor vessel for stolen continuance and an excellent doorway for things that dislike being dead.

Thermal exposure may awaken hungry batches. Distillate should not be warmed except under seal. It should not be cooled below lawful threshold unless the operator has been trained in shatter-continuance retrieval. It should not be placed near Liminal personnel undergoing heat-starvation, no matter how persuasive they become.

Auditory exposure is rare but documented. Certain batches sing. Certain batches answer. Certain batches repeat the last sound made by the source organism. If a vessel begins using the voice of a dead officer, the correct response is not conversation but evacuation.

The first symptom of dangerous exposure is often conviction.

The exposed person becomes convinced that the substance is necessary, familiar, owed, holy, medical, harmless, misunderstood, lonely, or already part of them.

That is when the restraints are applied.

Every major organ of the Concord claims Distillate by necessity and condemns everyone else's claim as appetite.

## XV. Distillate and Class: The Liquid Shape of Inequality

In doctrinal language, Distillate preserves civilization.

In practical language, Distillate reveals who civilization believes is worth preserving.

A noble lord receives a Draught course to retain ancient administrative memory, house stability, industrial contract continuity, and political balance. A line soldier receives a battlefield derivative if his mission remains incomplete. A factory savant receives a restorative if her knowledge cannot be replaced. A lower-stack child receives candle smoke if the family can afford wax. A penal corpse-handler receives Silencer paste so the work detail can continue.

This hierarchy is not accidental.

It is written into allocation law.

The official doctrine states that rare substrate must be directed toward the greatest continuity yield. The poor call this the arithmetic of leaving people dead. Logistica calls it triage. Null Court calls it settled. Cultus Vitae calls it tragic necessity. Noble houses call it civilization whenever the cup is in their hand.

Yet Distillate also undermines class order because desire moves downward as readily as authority moves upward.

Illegal derivatives make nobles dependent on lower-stack refiners. Salvage from battlefields enriches smugglers. Dead ampules become dowries. Candle-trace rumors move crowds. A starving district may riot not for bread but for access to a clinic said to contain one cracked vial. A noble house may fall because a servant learned which warming drawer held the true ampules and which held decoys.

Distillate is therefore not merely a substance.

It is a political weather system.

Where it appears, law thickens, money gathers, bodies disappear, hope becomes dangerous, and every institution discovers fresh reasons to lie.

## XVI. Known Distillate-Derived Preparations

The following preparations are included for identification, not replication.

Possession of this list without appropriate seal may be interpreted as intent, which has always been easier to prosecute than chemistry.

**Aurum Vitae:** lawful high-grade precursor used in Draught production. Appearance variable. Value catastrophic. Theft triggers multi-organ response.

**Grave Milk:** illegal pale suspension used in lower-stack fever dens. Often counterfeit. Genuine batches can close wounds while eroding memory of why the wound mattered.

**Longwake:** labor stimulant with fractional brightener content. Common in docks, convoy yards, and siege manufactories. Long-term use causes sleep debt hallucination, tooth loosening, and obedience rage.

**Saint Smoke:** inhalant produced from trace candle scrapings, black tallow, and ashglass dust. Produces warmth, grief softening, and chapel-memory euphoria. Also produces lung injury and susceptibility to false miracles.

**King's Mercy:** noble pain-killing cordial used during surgery, dueling recovery, and cosmetic age reversal. Lower-grade imitations kill by sweetness.

**Ashline Tincture:** frontier medicine used to stabilize shoreline exposure. Legal only under Liminal or Custodia Cineris seal. Smugglers sell weak versions to travelers who believe courage is a substitute for jurisdiction.

**Red Renewal:** battlefield injectable restricted to Aeteric Guard and selected Legio medicae. Restores function under catastrophic trauma. Side effects include aggression, identity narrowing, and post-action grief collapse.

**Mortis Binder:** clinical preparation used for Reversion control and death-interview stabilization. Not pleasant. Patients often survive with strong opinions regarding the definition of mercy.

**Candle-Trace Oil:** devotional scent oil containing trace or alleged trace substrate. Legally ambiguous in most districts. Economically enormous.

**Phage Gold:** weapon-grade hostile restorative contradiction. Forbidden outside sealed arsenals. If found in civilian possession, evacuate before arresting anyone ambitious enough to own it.

**Null-Washed Distillate:** Conceptual-suppressed substrate used in court-supervised remediation and certain weapons. Tasteless, gray-gold, and offensive to poets.

**Dead Batch Resin:** failed preparation sold as cheap narcotic or used in illegal machine rites. May do nothing. May remember the source organism incorrectly.

## XVII. Enforcement Notes for Field Officers

A field officer encountering suspected Distillate should not attempt heroism.

Heroism is an excellent way to become evidence.

Secure distance.

Do not taste.

Do not smell deliberately.

Do not warm.

Do not cool.

Do not pocket residue.

Do not permit religious personnel to bless it without seal confirmation.

Do not permit noble representatives to remove it under emergency inheritance privilege, which they will invent if not already available.

Do not permit machine-priests, Undying servants, or Ferrum-adjacent technicians to attach diagnostic apparatus without knowing which synod taught them what questions machines are allowed to ask.

Do not let Liminal personnel near hungry batches unless restrained, warmed by safe source, and supervised by an officer authorized to recognize ash-compulsion.

Do not allow the crowd to believe the substance is cure, relic, poison, ration, or proof of official murder until official murder has been ruled out by someone paid to distinguish it from policy.

If civilians have already been exposed, separate by symptom rather than rank unless rank arrives with armed escort. Record names and ident values immediately. Distillate exposure has been known to alter both.

If the substance sings, evacuate.

If the substance repeats a name, evacuate faster.

If the substance begins counting, do not wait to learn what number it respects.

## XVIII. Closing Doctrine: The Bottled Argument

Distillate is the Concord in miniature.

It is victory stolen from death and made into ration.

It is medicine that remembers predation.

It is sacrament cut with inventory law.

It is weaponized mercy.

It is noble privilege, battlefield necessity, industrial temptation, black-market dream, chapel fraud, and the alchemical shadow of the First Distiller falling endlessly into every cup poured after him.

The Concord teaches that Distillate must be controlled because uncontrolled continuance destroys worlds.

This is true.

The Concord also controls Distillate because whoever controls the drop controls who may continue, who must end, who may heal, who must work through pain, who may rise after death, who may be named strategically necessary, and who may be told there was not enough medicine.

This is also true.

Between these truths lies the entire empire.

Handle accordingly.
