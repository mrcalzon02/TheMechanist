The **Martyrs of Lightness** are Unburdened agents or victims whose deaths are staged, permitted, or shaped into public proof. They die calmly under fire, walk into flame smiling, dissolve into ash while singing, forgive executioners before sentence, or leave behind bodies so peaceful that witnesses begin envying them.

### Public Dissolution

A public dissolution may begin as execution, protest, hospice death, hunger strike, furnace refusal, or battlefield surrender. The body becomes light. The voice softens. Pain signs vanish. Bystanders feel warmth, relief, or sudden grief resolution. Ash may rise instead of fall. Names spoken over the dying may blur.

If witnesses are not contained, the event becomes a seed. Rumors improve it. Enemies soften it. Priests accidentally sanctify it by condemning it too loudly.
