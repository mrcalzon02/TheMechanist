**Renders** are the most direct and least subtle Bestiae phenotype. They breach, charge, tear, crush, shear, swallow, and scatter. Officers inexperienced with the Ash Sea often prefer Renders because their intentions are obvious. Experienced officers know obvious hunger can still be a tactic.

### Morphology

Renders commonly present as massive quadruped, hexaped, or crawling brutes with carbonized armor plates over red exposed interiors. Limbs may be jointed incorrectly. Heads may be numerous or absent. Mouths may open along shoulders, bellies, palms, or wounds. Many carry surfaces resembling burned battlefield metal fused to flesh. Their claws often leave wounds that continue widening after the strike.

### Behavior

The Render attacks structural confidence. Gates, shield lines, bunkers, armored vehicles, and heroic formations attract it. It prefers the places where soldiers believe mass will protect them. Its roar may carry remembered impact: artillery, collapsing towers, ship hull rupture, or the final sound of a herd species under predation.

Renders leave residue of low metaphysical value. This distinguishes them from Deep Grazers. They do not carefully harvest identity. They break the container and let subtler things feed afterward.

### Countermeasures

Use depth, heat, crossfire, and denial of straight corridors. Do not cluster around symbols. Do not hold heroic chokepoints longer than geometry requires. Renders enjoy monuments. Thermal lances and mass-driver percussion remain effective when applied before the unit begins naming the beast.

Field maxim: **If it wants the gate, make the gate unimportant.**
