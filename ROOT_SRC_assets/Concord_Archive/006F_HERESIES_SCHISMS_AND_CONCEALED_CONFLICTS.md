## PREFACE FOR BISHOPS WHO PREFER SIMPLER LIES

The Cultus Vitae is united in public because public disunity kills districts.

Privately, it has never been united for longer than the time required to write a declaration claiming that it is.

This record summarizes the principal schisms, heresies, and concealed conflicts within the Cultus Vitae. It is not to be copied into parish education, memorial sermons, or youth catechisms. Citizens require faith, not footnotes.

---

## THE WARMTH SCHISM

The Warmth Schism began as an argument over whether heat was symbol, sacrament, medical necessity, or a lesser form of divine presence.

In ordinary circumstances the question would have remained safely useless.

Then several Liminal-adjacent parishes began reporting that warmth rites reduced self-immolation attempts, black tear episodes, and ash-facing stupor. Furnace Abbeys adopted the rites. White Censer houses added perfumed heat vapors. Lower-stack citizens began sleeping in unauthorized shrine-boiler rooms.

The schism turned violent when one sect declared cold a moral failure.

The official settlement permits warmth as comfort, medicine, and symbol, but forbids its classification as divinity. The settlement is ignored wherever the pipes still work.

## THE MERCY-ENDING HERESY

Mercy-Ending teaches that life preserved beyond dignity becomes theft from the dead.

Its adherents arise most often among hospice clergy, plague attendants, and Red Basin veterans who have seen too many citizens repaired only to return to suffering.

The heresy is dangerous because it sounds compassionate and because it can be defended with examples no honest person enjoys refuting.

The Cult suppresses Mercy-Ending through reassignment, silence retreats, memory correction, and when necessary Ideological Knife review.

The Unburdened consider Mercy-Ending a doorway. The Cult considers it a crack in the wall.

## THE OPEN FURNACE DOCTRINE

Open Furnace sects teach that all bodies should be burned without hierarchy, noble exception, relic preservation, Mortis delay, or family negotiation.

They are beloved by workers who resent noble corpse privileges.

They are hated by nobles who have purchased elaborate post-mortem relevance.

They are watched by Mortis officials because rapid burning can destroy evidence, lineage claims, cineric signs, and inconvenient proof that someone died before signing a document.

The doctrine is partially correct, socially explosive, and therefore heretical in most jurisdictions.

## THE RETURNING DROP CONTROVERSY

Some Cult theologians argue that all Distillate, however diluted, retains a desire to reunite with its origin in the First Drop.

This doctrine produces troubling conclusions regarding medicine, holy candles, noble Draught consumption, restorative narcotics, and the possibility that every trace use participates in a distributed hunger older than the Concord.

The official position states that properly stabilized Distillate derivatives are chemically obedient.

The White Censer repeats this often. Repetition is sometimes used as disinfectant.

---

## THE CHILDREN'S ASH CONTROVERSY

The Order of the Children's Ash has repeatedly clashed with parish authorities over whether orphaned children belong primarily to surviving kin, civic labor boards, Cult custody, noble patronage, military recruitment, or Mortis-lineage preservation.

The public sees charity.

The sealed record sees custody wars.

In three known spires, Children's Ash houses concealed children from noble bloodline auditors. In two, the children were Liminal-positive. In one, the abbess was correct to hide them. In one, she doomed the lower district. In one, the file remains sealed because the answer would please no institution.

## THE QUIET CANON WAR

The Quiet Canon War was not a war in the Legio sense. No banners were raised, no front declared, and no citizen was informed why sermons changed.

For forty-six years, Preceptors aligned to rival doctrinal schools altered hymnals, funeral lines, hospice scripts, and child catechisms to shift the Cult toward competing visions of humanity's purpose.

The Continuance School preached endurance.

The Redemption School preached purification.

The Furnace School preached labor.

The Mercy School preached gentleness.

The Knife School preached that gentleness was useful until it became treason.

The war ended when the Custodia Veritatis demonstrated that seventeen altered hymns could induce coordinated lower-stack refusal to work during mourning week.

Several Preceptors were retired into concealed monasteries. Several vanished. The hymnals were corrected. Citizens were told the changes reflected improved meter.

## THE RED BASIN ACCUSATIONS

The Red Basin has been accused of harvesting useful tissue before legal death.

The Order denies this.

The Mortis audit found insufficient proof.

The Locked Vein audit found proof but insufficient jurisdiction.

The Null Court found jurisdiction but insufficient political appetite.

The Cult declared a period of renewed surgical humility and changed the wording on three consent forms.

The matter is closed except among families who can count fingers.

---

## THE IDEOLOGICAL KNIFE QUESTION

The greatest internal schism concerns the Ideological Assassins.

One school argues that the Cult cannot preach life while maintaining killers.

Another argues that life without killers is a sermon delivered to a throat already cut.

The official doctrine states that the Ideological Knife does not kill for the Church. It removes conditions under which humanity would be killed in greater number.

This distinction has preserved the order for centuries and the conscience of no one who understands it.

## CONCEALED CONFLICTS WITH OTHER INSTITUTIONS

With **Collegia Mortis**, the Cult contests who owns last words.

With **Concordia Logistica**, it contests whether a starving citizen needs bread or permission to hope for bread.

With **Eternus Legio**, it contests whether soldiers die as assets, martyrs, witnesses, or waste.

With **Ferrum Sanctum**, it contests whether machine preservation of life is holy care or mechanical blasphemy.

With **Liminal Office**, it contests whether ash-touched agony should be sanctified, treated, hidden, or used.

With **Null Court**, it contests whether correct law can ever become evil by accident.

With **Custodia Veritatis**, it contests whether truth itself requires pastoral authorization.
