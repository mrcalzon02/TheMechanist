# 005K CONCORDIA LOGISTICA SUPPORT NETWORK ROSTER
## Short Concordial Addendum Concerning Recognized Directorates, Auxiliary Arms, Infrastructure Courts, Mobile Factors, Dead Offices, and the Bloated Bureaucratic Corpse by Which the Concord Continues to Move

Filed beneath **005 Concordia Logistica** as a working roster for legates, district governors, noble stewards, Legio requisition officers, Mortis examiners, Ferrum auditors, Turris wardens, Liminal provisioning clerks, and citizens senior enough to fear the words *support network* when spoken by a Logistica factor.

This roster is not exhaustive.

No roster of the Logistica can be exhaustive because the Logistica has spent centuries creating offices to remember why prior offices failed, then creating new offices to interpret the memory, then sealing the old offices for reasons the new offices are no longer cleared to know.

The Concordia Logistica is often drawn as a circulatory system.

This is too flattering.

A circulatory system is clean in diagrams. The Logistica is an empire-sized corpse whose veins still work because clerks keep squeezing the right swollen places.

---

## I. PRINCIPAL ARMS OF MEASURE

**The Ration Directorate** — Calculates food entitlement, emergency reduction, famine priority, military ration, ceremonial distribution, and the terrible politics of who eats first when there is not enough.

**The Famine Courts** — Judicial ration organs empowered to criminalize hoarding, impose reduction, seize stores, condemn fraudulent distribution, and declare districts non-viable under extreme scarcity.

**The Water Measure Office** — Controls cistern writs, pump schedules, drought courts, potable classification, contamination notices, water priority, and the dangerous grammar of thirst.

**The Transit and Lift Authority** — Governs elevators, roads, bridges, freight corridors, corpse descent, military passage, noble ascent, refugee movement, route locks, and all violence caused by delay.

**The Manufactorum Flow Board** — Measures production, machine-time, raw material input, output truth, worker quotas, Undying Lord industrial claims, and Ferrum-contested automation boundaries.

**The Fleet Provisioning Synod** — Loads vessels for Ash-Sea transit, war, evacuation, pilgrimage, colony reinforcement, noble escape, corpse return, and other missions that become impossible when someone forgets water has weight.

**The Mortis Supply Liaison** — Allocates furnace fuel, body sacks, preservation salts, corpse carts, plague kits, memory caskets, contaminated labor supply, and the material needs of death kept from becoming civic rule.

**The Civic Identity Ledger** — Binds persons to ration, housing, tax, movement, inheritance, burial, arrest, entitlement, and the many disasters caused by names behaving badly.

**The Seal Banks** — Issue, validate, revoke, duplicate, dispute, and guard seals, permits, identity marks, requisition codes, and other authority tokens that transform desire into lawful consequence.

**The Depot Reliquaries and Reserve Vaults** — Preserve emergency stores, granaries, armories, machine parts, medical stock, holy consumables, obsolete tools, and the material memory of past terror.

**The Permit Courts and Procurement Seal Houses** — Adjudicate purchase, seizure, contract writ, requisition, noble supply claim, emergency taking, and the distinction between theft and paperwork.

**The Convoy Factors and Route Wardens** — Carry ledgers across roads, dead-world lanes, ash routes, bridges, outer districts, and contested corridors where stationary law is a rumor with boots.

## II. AUXILIARY AND REGIONAL SUPPORT BODIES

**The Bridge Weight Conservators** — Maintain load limits, route fatigue records, and old crossing rights. Universally ignored until a bridge collapses, at which point they become prophets everyone should have obeyed.

**The Candle and Ceremonial Wax Office** — Regulates holy candle distribution, noble mourning wax, Cultus Vitae fragrance claims, and alleged fractional distillate particle content in sanctioned devotional consumables.

**The Spare Part Census** — Tracks interchangeable, non-interchangeable, allegedly interchangeable, and fatally similar components across factories, ships, pumps, lifts, and weapons.

**The Ash-Glass Allocation Desk** — Assigns ash glass for windows, containment, furnace shielding, Mortis vessels, noble conservatories, breach barriers, and fraudulent luxury work disguised as safety.

**The Queue Discipline Bureau** — Designs waiting-line architecture, token sequence, crowd gates, and ration-square flow. Mocked by soldiers until stampedes begin.

**The Emergency Blanket Compact** — A minor but durable office whose blankets have appeared in almost every evacuation since the Third Continuity Consolidation, including two where no one had requested them and one where they were the only correctly routed supplies.

**The Lost Pallet Recovery Office** — Exists because goods vanish inside bureaucracy as often as on roads. Its clerks are feared by warehouse thieves and loved by desperate hospitals.

**The Weight and Measure Orthodoxy Board** — Maintains unit standards. Its civil wars over obsolete measures have delayed artillery, bread, and once a noble wedding cake large enough to require structural review.

**The Spoilage Choir** — Not a religious body despite the name. A group of rot clerks whose spoken inspections of grain, meat, fungus, and medicinal stock acquired ritual form because repetition reduces error and terror.

**The Archive of Apologies Not Yet Issued** — An unofficial Logistica phrase for sealed correspondence prepared in advance of expected failure. Several such apologies have become binding policy after being discovered by victims.

## III. DEAD, HALF-DEAD, AND UNHELPFULLY ALIVE OFFICES

**The Bureau of Siege Turnips** — Formally abolished. Still receives three reports per year from agricultural districts that have never grown turnips but fear discontinuing the form.

**The Third Office of Coal-Salt Substitution** — Absorbed into Manufactorum Flow. Its seal remains valid in two outer sectors and invalid in seven, depending on whether the local index was updated after the fourth correction.

**The Lift Bell Reconciliation Chamber** — Created to standardize emergency bell codes. Failed. Its surviving bell tables are now used by smugglers, three noble houses, and one respectable hospital.

**The Office of Temporary Permanent Camps** — Established during refugee consolidation. Its camps became districts. Its districts became taxable. Its office became politically inconvenient and therefore technically temporary forever.

**The Dry Grain Saints' Ledger** — A Cultus-Logistica hybrid office that classified miraculous grain preservation. Closed after Ferrum auditors discovered ordinary chemical treatment. Reopened after the same treatment failed during an ash bloom and the grain remained edible anyway.

**The Heliun Machine Replacement Claims Desk** — Formed after the Divide to compensate offices for destroyed cognition systems. Never closed because some offices are still discovering which old machines were doing work no living clerk understood.

## IV. DOCTRINE OF THE BUREAUCRATIC CORPSE

The Logistica's many organs are often accused of bloat. The accusation is accurate and incomplete.

The Logistica is bloated because the Concord is old, frightened, wounded, over-layered, under-supplied, legally haunted, infrastructurally contradictory, and unwilling to forget any rule that once prevented catastrophe. Each office began as an answer. Each answer became a procedure. Each procedure became a jurisdiction. Each jurisdiction became a budget, a seal, a rivalry, a hallway, a cadre of clerks, a private language, and eventually a corpse that still signs forms.

Reformers regularly propose simplification.

The Logistica buries many of them in advisory committees and a few in excellent footnotes.

Yet the corpse cannot simply be burned. Hidden in its swollen tissues are forgotten routes, obsolete tools, famine exceptions, bridge keys, old plague methods, dead-world maps, and emergency authorities that have repeatedly saved populations when elegant modern systems failed. The corpse is diseased. It is also an archive of survival.

Therefore the Logistica doctrine is not purity.

It is managed necrosis.

Cut away too little and rot spreads.

Cut away too much and the empire bleeds out through the missing paperwork.

## V. FIELD CAUTION

Any officer interacting with Concordia Logistica support networks shall remember the following:

A clerk may outrank a general if the clerk controls fuel.

A wrong seal may starve a loyal district.

An obsolete form may open a road no living official remembers owning.

A minor office may be ridiculous until the day its ridiculous stockpile becomes the only surviving supply.

A queue is architecture.

A warehouse is politics.

A ration is law.

A convoy is a moving lawsuit.

A name is a supply line tied to a body.

The Logistica does not make civilization noble.

It makes civilization continue long enough for nobility, cruelty, worship, treason, hunger, and hope to stand in line together.
