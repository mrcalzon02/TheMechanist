**Herders** are Bestiae whose primary function is movement control. They may kill few citizens directly while causing mass death by driving crowds into the wrong place. They are the predator's logistics corps.

### Morphology

Herders often appear lean, many-limbed, smoke-flanked, horned by memory rather than bone, or split into several half-seen bodies moving along rooftops, vents, and side streets. Their eyes are frequently lateral, like animals adapted to watch a flock. Witnesses report the smell of hot dust and old panic.

### Behavior

The Herder manipulates flight. It appears where it must be seen and vanishes where it wants civilians to run. It blocks safe roads with terror and leaves dangerous roads apparently clear. It uses screams, minor wounds, false fires, and glimpsed movement to shape crowds.

Herders often operate before Black Tide events, Red Shepherd contamination, or Conceptual rescue-mask manifestations. A crowd driven by a Herder may believe it chose every turn.

### Countermeasures

Evacuation officers must distrust open paths. Use preplanned route locks, aerial observation, and independent map teams. If all citizens are fleeing away from the visible monster, determine what they are fleeing toward. Cohortes Ferrata should collapse attractive corridors before they become funnels.

Field maxim: **The beast behind the crowd may be less dangerous than the road ahead.**
