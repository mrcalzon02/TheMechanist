# 017I UNDYING LORDS SYNOD ROSTER
## Short Concordial Addendum Concerning Recognized, Suspect, Extinct, and Disputed Synods Within the Machine-Noble Continuance Body

Filed beneath the parent seal of **017 Undying Lords and Mechanical Continuance** as a working roster for Ferrum inspectors, Null Court inheritance clerks, Concordia Logistica industrial assessors, Collegia Mortis continuity examiners, and Legio officers who must know which machine noble is likely to answer a simple question with a sermon, a lawsuit, a scalpel, or artillery.

This roster is not exhaustive.

No roster of Undying Lord synods can be exhaustive unless the compiler is immortal, immune to bribery, capable of reading sealed house ledgers, and willing to survive dinner with thirteen machine aristocrats who each believe the other twelve are inefficient heretics.

The following synods and doctrinal bodies are recognized for field use:

---

## I. Principal Continuance Synods

**The Synod of Mechanical Excision** — Holds that flesh is weakness and that identity is strengthened by cutting away biological failure. Dominant among severe industrial houses, siege foundries, furnace dynasties, and high-efficiency command estates.

**The Wet-Core Preservationists** — Maintain living biological anchors within mechanical support systems. They argue that personhood requires warm tissue, pain, metabolism, and embodied vulnerability. Their organ-debt ledgers require constant inspection.

**The Electro-Hollow Synods** — Construct constrained memory shells, voice reliquaries, advisory hollows, and ancestral response vessels while insisting these are not digital persons. Permanent Heliun-risk classification applies.

**The Electroquant Neural Tappers** — Preserve decision cadence through high-resolution neural sampling. Favored by military-industrial houses and command lineages. Useful, cold, and prone to mistaking executive function for the whole soul.

**The Shidnan Soul-Form Cartographers** — Map continuity outlines through metaphysical, neurological, behavioral, and ash-resonant indicators. Valuable during succession disputes and continuance compatibility assessments. Dangerous when they decide the map can become the traveler.

**The Foundry Patrician Compact** — Not a single doctrine but a coalition of factory barons whose authority rests upon ownership of critical production knowledge. Their theology is often inventory wearing ceremonial iron.

**The Heliun Remainder Watch** — Officially nonexistent. Unofficially active wherever old cognition systems, digital remnants, suspicious hollows, or machine-mind residues remain useful enough that somebody hesitates to destroy them.

---

## II. Secondary, Regional, and Disputed Synods

**The Brass Lung Communion** — Specializes in breath engines, pressure sanctums, lung-caskets, and atmospheric continuance. Common in poisoned spires, ash-adjacent cities, and noble houses whose ancestors died badly of air.

**The Casket Choirs of Nhal** — Maintain multiple partial continuants in harmonic advisory assemblies. The Collegia Mortis dislikes their definitions of individual consent. The Null Court dislikes their voting procedures.

**The Iron Appetite School** — Teaches that hunger, desire, and bodily compulsion should be mechanically simulated after excision to preserve ambition. Frequently produces lords who no longer need food but still consume districts.

**The Memory Riveters** — Preserve knowledge through manual repetition, scar-coded apprenticeships, tool inheritance, and flesh-machine labor discipline rather than direct cognitive capture. Respected by factory workers, patronized by barons, underestimated by everyone wearing cleaner gloves.

**The Chrome Widow Orders** — Spousal continuance houses built around preserving dead partners as household authority engines. Often legally disastrous. Often politically effective. Not recommended for mediation without Mortis observers.

**The Quiet Bearing Fellowship** — A minimalist school that preserves only essential motor and process instincts. Used in manufactories where a dead master’s hand motion matters more than his opinions. Ferrum classification pending.

**The Sainted Gear Lineages** — Families that claim certain machines contain sanctified ancestral presence without formal hollow construction. Half religion, half warranty fraud, occasionally correct.

**The Severed Eye Observatories** — Continuance houses specializing in preserved perception, surveillance organs, command optics, and remote industrial sight. Frequently entangled with Custodia Veritatis suspicion.

**The Marrow Contract Houses** — Wet-core offshoots that treat compatible marrow as dynastic currency. Heavily litigated. Heavily armed.

**The Ash-Salt Metallurgists** — Claim that identity can be stabilized through trace Cineric exposure during metal integration. Most branches are censured. A few remain protected by results nobody wants to admit are impressive.

**The Thrice-Locked Mnemonic Banks** — Pre-Heliun archive houses that store human memory in non-generative mechanical vaults. Officially safe. Unofficially inspected whenever a vault starts answering too quickly.

**The Null Temperance Synod** — Argues that true continuance requires refusal of all emotional simulation after preservation. Produces stable administrators and unbearable relatives.

**The Reductive Mercy Houses** — Excisionist reformers who claim biological reduction should be performed only to relieve suffering. Their enemies call them hypocrites; their patients sometimes call them saviors.

**The Crown of Last Instruction** — Deathbed knowledge-capture synod that extracts final operational memories from dying experts. Widely used, widely feared, and forbidden from entering hospitals without posted notice in three provinces.

**The Glass Nerve Society** — Uses transparent nerve-vats for public verification of living continuity. Popular among houses accused of false preservation. Disturbing at banquets.

**The Locked Smile School** — Cosmetic-preservation faction that maintains human faces over extreme internal replacement. Common in diplomatic machine houses. The Ferrum warns that a familiar smile proves nothing.

## III. Extinct or Censured Bodies

**The Heliun Digitalist Synods** — Destroyed or driven into denial after the Heliun Divide. Advocated full electronic continuance, digital personhood, and non-corporeal survival. Their names remain under censure.

**The Open Server Choirs** — Believed copied minds could remain loyal if raised in supervised community. Their population mathematics became evidence against them.

**The Self-Correcting Ancestor Houses** — Permitted preserved command engines to alter themselves without living witness. All known branches censured; several suspected to have become Remainder Watch fronts.

**The Thousand Vessel Doctrine** — Claimed identity strengthened through multiplication rather than weakened by it. Ferrum law classifies surviving texts as ignition-grade heresy.

**The Gentle Fork Communion** — Claimed that a copied mind could be considered a child rather than duplicate. The term child remains prohibited in Heliun proceedings.

---

## IV. Practical Field Guidance

If the house smells of oil, heated metal, and disinfected courage, expect excisionists.

If the halls are warm, damp, perfumed, and full of relatives speaking softly to tanks, expect wet-core preservationists.

If the dead answer questions too politely, expect Electro-Hollows.

If every conversation feels like an examination, expect Neural Tappers.

If the walls are covered in diagrams of people who are not present, expect Shidnans.

If nobody discusses theology until production quotas are threatened, expect Foundry Patricians.

If everyone insists there are no forbidden devices on site before anyone asks, expect the Heliun Remainder Watch.

Final roster note: synods change names frequently after censure, scandal, industrial disaster, inheritance embarrassment, failed resurrection, successful resurrection, or the sudden discovery that their founding doctrine has become taxable. Inspectors shall therefore treat this roster as a beginning, not a shield.

The Undying Lords do not merely preserve themselves.

They preserve arguments.

Some of the arguments have guns.
