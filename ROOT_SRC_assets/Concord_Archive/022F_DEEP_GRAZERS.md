**Deep Grazers** consume memory, name-anchoring, learned skill, emotional specificity, and identity structure before consuming flesh, if they consume flesh at all. They are quiet disasters. A Render creates corpses. A Deep Grazer creates strangers wearing correct bodies.

### Morphology

Deep Grazers are often large, slow, dimly luminous beneath ash film, and surrounded by whispering particulate. Their mouths may be hidden. Their feeding surfaces are frequently hair, fronds, veils, or soft tendrils that brush rather than bite. Some resemble grazing animals because the mind insists on calling slow feeding peaceful.

### Behavior

Victims return alive but cannot attach meaning to names, tools, children, debts, or wounds. They may know how to speak but not why any sentence matters. Skilled workers forget craft sequence while remembering slogans. Soldiers remember drill but not comrades. Mothers recognize infants as objects requiring care but lack the personal heat of recognition.

Deep Grazer events can collapse a district without visible battle because everyone remains present and no one remains socially complete.

### Countermeasures

Immediate memory anchoring: legal name, tactile object, recent pain, strong smell, living witness, and contradictory questions. Kill or drive off the Grazer with heat and sonic disruption. Do not allow affected populations to perform critical work until identity testing is complete.

Field maxim: **A body that returns without its meanings is not evidence of mercy.**
