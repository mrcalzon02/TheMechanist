The **Painless Doctors** are Unburdened medical vectors. Some are trained physicians. Some are failed Mortis assistants. Some are grieving relatives who learned a forbidden touch. Some are not human enough by the time they are caught for credentials to matter.

They thrive where official medicine is expensive, rationed, noble-prioritized, or humiliating. They offer relief without questions. They do not ask for ident codes. They do not report illegal births, unsanctioned names, ration fraud, contamination fear, or unpaid medical debts. They remove pain first and doctrine later.

### Methods

Known methods include ash-cool poultices, breath synchronization, low chants beneath hearing range, black-salt injections, dream-sharing, communal fever rooms, and hand contact that reduces agony while lowering name response. Some treatments are chemically active. Others are Conceptual. The patient rarely cares while the pain is ending.

### Hazard

Pain is not sacred, but it is an anchor. A subject stripped of pain without replacement anchors may drift toward lightening. In hospice settings, entire wards have accepted release together after reporting the presence of a kind physician whose face no surviving nurse could describe.

The worst Painless Doctors do not murder. They make dying beautiful enough that the living volunteer to become patients.

### Countermeasures

Provide lawful pain relief quickly. Audit clinics without contempt. Separate healer from doctrine where possible. Some early Painless Doctors can be recovered if given supplies, status, and supervision before ash empathy crosses the dooring threshold.

Hospice Hands beyond threshold are not to be touched skin to skin. Gloves must be heated, named, and destroyed after use.

Field maxim: **Pain relief is mercy; painlessness without self is harvest.**
