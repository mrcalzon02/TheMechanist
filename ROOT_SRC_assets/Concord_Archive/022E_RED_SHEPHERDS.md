# 022E — RED SHEPHERDS

**Red Shepherds** are among the most strategically dangerous Bestiae phenotypes because they understand the value of survivors. They wound without killing, mark without consuming, and allow the marked to return home carrying heat, grief, dreams, eggs of phrase, ash-blood, or invisible breach correspondence.

### Morphology

Red Shepherds commonly exhibit raw red interiors under blackened ribs, long guiding limbs, multiple small mouths along the flank, and hands rather than claws at the end of some appendages. They may appear almost gentle while directing victims away from immediate death.

### Behavior

They separate one survivor from a doomed group, inflict a memorable wound, show the survivor a vision, then allow escape along an apparently difficult path. The survivor becomes credible because survival seems earned. Later symptoms include warmth at the wound, dreams of guiding others, compulsion to return to the site, and phrases beginning with *I know the way*.

### Countermeasures

All improbable survivors of Bestiae contact are quarantined without accusation. Wounds are mapped, heated, named, and inspected for memory response. Families are not permitted unsupervised reunion until after third-night dream audit. This policy is hated. Red Shepherd outbreaks explain why it continues.

Field maxim: **A spared witness may be a leash.**
