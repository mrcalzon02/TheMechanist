# CULTUS VITAE — ORDERS OF CARE AND RESTORATION
## Registry of Medical, Charitable, and Survivalist Arms


> Classification: Cultus Vitae Internal Register / Concordial Continuity Seal
> Circulation: episcopal, Mortis, Logistica-hospice, Legio chaplaincy, and Null Court audit bodies only
> Public Copy: denied except by parish abstraction
> Doctrine Warning: the Cultus Vitae is not to be described as a voluntary association. It is a civil organ of continuity expressed through worship.


---

## PURPOSE OF THIS REGISTRY

The Cultus Vitae maintains more support orders than can be counted without inviting a Logistica clerk to die of satisfaction.

This registry lists the principal care and restoration bodies recognized by the Living Canon. It excludes purely local charities, temporary famine brotherhoods, battlefield miracle cults, unlawful hospice sects, and orders whose existence remains sealed under Ideological Knife authority.

---

## ORDER OF THE RED BASIN

The Red Basin is the oldest surgical order still permitted to operate under Cult seal.

Its members are trained to treat wounds produced by factories, battlefields, breach events, penal labor, failed noble augmentation, and certain forms of Cineric contact where the patient's continued life has not yet become legally embarrassing.

Their symbol is a basin beneath a flame.

Their motto is: **Spill nothing that may yet serve.**

Critics call them butchers with hymns. Survivors call them mother, father, saint, monster, and debt-holder depending on how much of them was saved.

---

## ORDER OF THE WHITE CENSER

The White Censer compounds approved medicines, inhalants, grief sedatives, stimulants, disinfectant smokes, and holy fragrances containing legally meaningless traces of substances too expensive to name.

They operate behind clean masks and dirtier ledgers.

Their laboratories are attached to monasteries, noble hospice suites, fleet chapels, and pilgrim quarantine halls. They are the reason a starving district can be kept calm for three more days and the reason a noble can survive an injury that would have been called martyrdom in a worker.

The order is regularly audited for Distillate diversion.

The audit success rate is excellent because the White Censer helps write the audit procedures.

---

## ORDER OF SAINT MERROW'S LAMP

Saint Merrow's Lamp maintains deathwatch clergy who sit beside the dying until witness is complete.

They are trained to distinguish confession from delirium, prophecy from trauma, heresy from pain, and valuable testimony from the ordinary bargaining of a frightened soul.

They carry small lamps with shielded flames.

The flame is shown to the dying and then hidden if panic worsens.

Some citizens claim the Lamp steals last words for the Cult. This is slander. The Lamp records last words for authorized institutions, which is different in every way that matters to a lawyer.

---

## ORDER OF THE FURNACE MIDWIVES

The Furnace Midwives attend births under conditions of ration failure, bombardment, ash exposure, forced relocation, prison transport, noble succession secrecy, and Liminal bloodline custody.

They carry birth ledgers, warming cloths, emergency naming tablets, and small knives whose sanctity is disputed.

Their greatest power is not delivery but recognition. A Midwife who declares a child viable forces the Logistica to count it, the Mortis to wait, and the family to hope.

Hope at birth is dangerous. It causes people to demand tomorrow.

The Cult tolerates the danger because tomorrow requires citizens.

---

## ORDER OF THE COVERED FACE

The Covered Face serves in plague houses, ash quarantine wards, chemical exposure pits, and zones where survivors are too contaminated to embrace but too human to abandon.

They remove their names from public rolls before assignment. Their families are informed that service is honorable and visitation impossible.

Their faces are veiled not merely to avoid contagion but to prevent patients from attaching love to a rescuer who will probably die before the patient does.

The veils are burned at death. The names remain sealed. The work continues.

---

## ORDER OF THE LAST CUP

The Last Cup distributes controlled liquids during siege, famine, desert transit, and water-law crisis.

Its chalices are measured. Its mercy is counted. Its clergy are hated in any district where thirst has become arithmetic.

The order exists because water given freely can destroy a ration plan, and water withheld without ritual can destroy a government.

They teach citizens to sip under prayer because prayer slows the hand.

---

## ORDER OF THE CHILDREN'S ASH

This order maintains orphan refectories, child registries, grief dormitories, and emergency custody cloisters.

They are beloved by citizens who never needed them and feared by those who did.

A child taken by the order may live, learn, eat, and become useful. A child left outside the order may vanish into labor markets, cults, gangs, ration alleys, noble experiments, or the ash.

The choice is simple in doctrine and unforgivable in memory.

---

## ORDER OF THE LOCKED VEIN

The Locked Vein handles blood, marrow, organ, glandular tissue, and emergency biological reserves.

Their work is necessary, intimate, profitable, and spiritually nauseating.

They coordinate with Mortis, Logistica, Vast Medica Wings, noble recovery suites, penal infirmaries, and battlefield reclamation authorities. They maintain consent forms no sane person reads during crisis and no powerful person signs without amendment.

The order is one of the most watched bodies within the Cultus Vitae. This has not made it clean. It has made it careful.

---

## ORDER OF THE WARM WALL

The Warm Wall is a shelter order that maintains heated parish halls during freeze, power interruption, breach evacuation, and Liminal despair events.

It is the only Cultus order whose primary sacrament is temperature.

Citizens mock them until the heat fails.

Then the line forms around the block, and the priest at the door decides which old man, fevered mother, ash-touched child, armed deserter, and noble servant may enter first.

No theological school has produced a satisfying doctrine for that doorway.

---

## INTERNAL RIVALRIES

The Red Basin accuses the White Censer of perfumed cowardice.

The White Censer accuses the Red Basin of making medicine smell like panic.

The Last Cup distrusts the Warm Wall because warmth increases thirst.

The Locked Vein distrusts everyone because everyone eventually needs something from a body.

The Children's Ash distrusts the Furnace Midwives because every saved child becomes another mouth they may inherit.

The Living Canon calls these rivalries evidence of human frailty. The Custodia calls them map coordinates.

---

## CLOSING ENTRY

Care is never innocent inside a besieged civilization.

Every bandage decides what may continue. Every sedative decides what may be endured. Every hospice bed delays a corpse transfer. Every saved child becomes a future claim upon a ration table.

The Cultus Vitae is merciful.

This is why it is terrifying.
